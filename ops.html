Reference list on open quantum systems

Semiclassical Lindblad

  1. The ℏ → 0 limit of open quantum systems with general Lindbladians: vanishing noise ensures classicality beyond the Ehrenfest time, Felipe Hernández, Daniel Ranard, C. Jess Riedel, arXiv:2307.05326.

Tensor Methods for Density Matrices

  1. Quantum dynamics of thermalizing systems, Christopher David White, Michael Zaletel, Roger S. K. Mong, and Gil Refael, Phys. Rev. B 97, 035127.

HEOM(pole expansion, numerical instability)

  1. Universal time-domain Prony fitting decomposition for optimized hierarchical quantum master equations, Zi-Hao Chen, Yao Wang, Xiao Zheng, Rui-Xue Xu, and YiJing Yan, J. Chem. Phys. 156, 221102 (2022).
  2. Efficient low-temperature simulations for fermionic reservoirs with the hierarchical equations of motion method: Application to the Anderson impurity model, Xiaohan Dan, Meng Xu, J. T. Stockburger, J. Ankerhold, and Qiang Shi, Phys. Rev. B 107, 195429.
  3. Taming Quantum Noise for Efficient Low Temperature Simulations of Open Quantum Systems, Meng Xu, Yaming Yan, Qiang Shi, J. Ankerhold, and J. T. Stockburger, Phys. Rev. Lett. 129, 230601.
  4. About the performance of perturbative treatments of the spin-boson dynamics within the hierarchical equations of motion approach, arXiv:2308.02010.
  5. Removing instabilities in the hierarchical equations of motion: Exact and approximate projection approaches, J. Chem. Phys. 150, 184109 (2019).

Semiclassical theory of Non-Hermitian dynamics

  1. Wave-packet evolution in non-Hermitian quantum systems, Eva-Maria Graefe and Roman Schubert, Phys. Rev. A 83, 060101
  2. Non-Hermitian propagation of Hagedorn wavepackets, Caroline Lasser, Roman Schubert and Stephanie Troppmann, arXiv:1507.02858
  3. Semiclassical methods for investigating open quantum systems and decoherence, Tom Plastow, thesis.
  4. Semiclassical Phase-space Methods for Hermitian and non-Hermitian Quantum Dynamics, Alexander Rush, thesis.
